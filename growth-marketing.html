Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between growth hacking and digital marketing?

Digital marketing focuses on executing campaigns across channels. Growth hacking is a systematic process of experimentation to find the most efficient ways to grow. We often combine both approaches.

How long before I see results?

Some experiments generate immediate insights within weeks. Building a full growth system typically takes 3-6 months to show significant, measurable impact.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We have specific growth packages for startups with limited budgets, focusing on high-impact, low-cost experiments.

How do you measure success?

We measure success by your chosen North Star metric — monthly active users, revenue growth, customer acquisition cost, or whatever matters most to your business.

Will you train my team?

Yes. We build growth capability in your team through workshops, frameworks, and hands-on collaboration. Our goal is to make you self-sufficient.

What's included in the free growth audit?

A 60-minute deep dive into your funnel, current metrics, channels, and growth opportunities. You'll leave with 3 high-impact experiments to run immediately.
