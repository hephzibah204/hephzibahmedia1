# Hephzibah Media: Fractional CMO Marketing Plan
*Generated: June 2026 | Horizon: 12 Months*

## 1. Executive Summary

**The Situation:** Hephzibah Media has a 12-year track record, 200+ successful projects, and a 98% satisfaction rate. However, the business is currently generating only 10-15 leads per month because its digital presence has functioned historically as an informational brochure rather than a growth asset. 

**The Objective:** Transform Hephzibah Media into a lead-generation ecosystem targeting 40-60 highly qualified leads per month, driving ₦324M - ₦576M+ in annualized revenue potential across three core pillars: Tech/Web, Marketing, and Print.

**The 3 Big Bets (Next 90 Days):**
1. **The "WhatsApp Routing" Advantage:** Capitalize on Nigerian mobile behavior (85% of traffic) by routing mobile ad and organic traffic directly into WhatsApp via pre-filled messaging, drastically reducing form abandonment.
2. **Local SEO Dominance (Ogun/Lagos):** Exploit the geographical gap left by generic Lagos agencies by dominating local search ("Web design Ogun State," "Large format printing Ogere") while maintaining national authority.
3. **The "End-to-End" Brand Narrative:** Position explicitly against fragmented freelancer networks and generic DIY builders by hammering the message: *Strategy, Code, Marketing, and Print under one roof.*

**12-Month Outcome:** A predictable, automated client acquisition machine that relies less on founder-led hustle and more on systematic, data-driven funnels, establishing Hephzibah as the undisputed premium agency in South-West Nigeria.

## 4. Acquisition (How strangers become aware)

**Current State:** Relying on word-of-mouth and legacy organic presence.
**The Goal:** Build two parallel engines: a slow-burn compounding SEO engine and a fast-burn targeted Ads engine.

**90-Day Moves:**
1. **Deploy Programmatic Local SEO (Skill: `programmatic-seo`, `seo-audit`)**
   - Execute the Keyword Strategy (Document A & B).
   - *Tactic:* Publish the 3 planned pillar blog posts ("Why Website Speed Matters for Nigerian Businesses", etc.) and internally link to the newly optimized service pages. 
2. **Launch "Growth Asset" Meta Ads Campaign (Skill: `ads`, `ad-creative`)**
   - *Tactic:* Run targeted Facebook/Instagram ads aimed at SME owners in Lagos and Ogun. 
   - *Hook:* "Is your website an asset or a liability? We build growth engines."
   - *Routing:* Direct mobile ad clicks straight to the newly configured WhatsApp funnel.
3. **Directory & Local Listings (Skill: `directory-submissions`)**
   - *Tactic:* Ensure Hephzibah Media is listed accurately across all Nigerian business directories (VConnect, Finelib) and aggressively optimize the Google Business Profile for the Ogere location.

**12-Month Moves:**
- Launch the "Hephzibah Growth Podcast" or YouTube channel targeting Nigerian business digitalization.
- Scale LinkedIn outbound for corporate printing and custom software contracts.

## 5. Activation (Aware → First valued experience)

**Current State:** 10-15 leads/month. High friction on legacy forms.
**The Goal:** 40-60 leads/month via frictionless capture.

**90-Day Moves:**
1. **The WhatsApp "Fast Lane" (Skill: `cro`, `signup`)**
   - *Tactic:* Monitor the newly deployed mobile-to-WhatsApp redirect. Ensure the sales team (or founder) has a standardized, 5-minute response SLA (Service Level Agreement) for these inbound chats.
2. **Deploy the "Website ROI Calculator" Free Tool (Skill: `free-tools`)**
   - *Tactic:* Build and embed a simple interactive calculator (Traffic x Conv. Rate = Lost Revenue) on the homepage to capture email leads from visitors who aren't ready to book a call yet.
3. **Training Division Pre-Launch Waitlist (Skill: `launch`)**
   - *Tactic:* Before finalizing all curriculum, put up a waitlist capture on `/training.html` to validate demand for specific courses (e.g., UI/UX vs. SEO).

## 6. Retention (Repeat business & Retainers)

**Current State:** Project-based. 
**The Goal:** Increase Lifetime Value (LTV) by transitioning project clients into retainer clients.

**90-Day Moves:**
1. **The "Post-Launch" Upsell Sequence (Skill: `emails`)**
   - *Tactic:* 30 days after a Web Design project goes live, automatically send a "Protect Your Investment" email pitching the ₦50,000/mo Maintenance or ₦100,000/mo SEO retainer.
2. **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)**
   - *Tactic:* For any client spending over ₦500k, schedule a free 30-minute QBR every 3 months to discuss their metrics and pitch "Next Step" services (e.g., "We built the site, now let's drive traffic with Ads").

---

## 7. Referral (Retained users → More users)

**Current State:** Organic word-of-mouth (98% satisfaction rate).
**The Goal:** Systematize word-of-mouth.

**90-Day Moves:**
1. **The "Agency Partner" Affiliate Program (Skill: `referrals`)**
   - *Tactic:* Create a formalized referral structure. Offer a 10% cash finder's fee (or service credit) to existing clients who refer a new Web Design or Retainer client. 
2. **Automated Review Generation**
   - *Tactic:* Add a step to the project offboarding process: When a project is marked "Complete" and the client expresses satisfaction, automatically send a direct link to the Google Business Profile to capture a 5-star review.

## 8. Revenue (Monetization & Expansion)

**Current State:** ₦150,000 Starter to ₦350,000 Professional tiers.
**The Goal:** Increase Average Contract Value (ACV).

**90-Day Moves:**
1. **Launch the "Sales Funnels" Service (Skill: `launch`, `pricing`)**
   - *Tactic:* Position `/landing-pages-sales-funnels.html` as a premium, high-ROI service distinct from standard web design. Price this based on value (revenue generated) rather than hours worked.
2. **Productize the Print Division**
   - *Tactic:* Instead of custom quoting every flyer, create standardized "Startup Brand Kits" (Logo + 500 Cards + Letterhead + 2 Rollups) at a fixed, highly profitable price point to speed up the sales cycle.

## 13. Measurement & Open Decisions

**North-Star Metric:** Number of Qualified Leads Captured (Target: 40-60/mo).

**Leading Indicators:**
1. WhatsApp Initiation Rate (Mobile traffic to chat).
2. Cost Per Lead (CPL) on Meta Ads.
3. Organic Traffic Growth (Google Search Console).

**Open Decisions (For the Founder):**
- *Ad Budget:* Are we willing to commit ₦50,000 - ₦100,000/month to fuel the Meta Ads test engine?
