Frequently Asked Questions

How much does graphics design cost in Nigeria?

Pricing varies based on scope and complexity. Our Starter package begins at ₦80,000 for logo and basic collateral. We also offer custom packages. Chat with us for a tailored quote.

How long does a brand identity project take?

Logo and basic branding takes 1-2 weeks. Complete brand identity systems take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How many revision rounds are included?

Starter includes 2 rounds, Professional includes 3 rounds, and Premium includes unlimited revisions during the project phase. We work until you're satisfied.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive print-ready files (PDF, AI, EPS), web-optimized files (PNG, SVG, JPG), and brand guidelines in PDF format. All source files are included.

Can you design packaging for my product?

Yes! We specialize in packaging design that stands out on Nigerian shelves. Our Premium package includes packaging, or it can be added as a standalone service.

Do you offer ongoing design support?

Yes! Our Premium package includes 6 months of design support. We also offer monthly retainer plans for businesses that need regular design work.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans: 50% upfront, balance on completion. Custom arrangements available. Chat with us about what works for your budget.

What if I'm not happy with the design?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. We include multiple revision rounds to get it perfect. If you're not happy, we'll keep working until you are within the project scope.
