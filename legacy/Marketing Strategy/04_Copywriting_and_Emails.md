# Copywriting & Email Sequences Framework

*Based on 'copywriting' and 'emails' skills.*

## 1. Copywriting Framework (PAS & AIDA)
To transition from a "brochure" website to a "sales" website, all copy must follow proven direct-response frameworks.

### The PAS Framework (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
**Use Case:** Service Page Introductions and Facebook/LinkedIn Ads.
- **Problem:** "Is your website sitting there looking pretty but generating zero leads?"
- **Agitation:** "Every day you rely purely on paid ads or cold calling, you're bleeding money while your competitors capture the digital market. Your expensive website is basically a digital ghost town."
- **Solution:** "Hephzibah Media transforms your site into a High-Converting Lead Machine. We map user psychology to our 9-section template, complete with automated WhatsApp routing so you capture leads while they are hot."

### The AIDA Framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
**Use Case:** Training Course Landing Pages.
- **Attention:** "Want to double your income as a highly-paid Digital Marketer in 90 days?"
- **Interest:** "Companies in Nigeria are desperate for talent who can actually generate ROI, not just post on social media."
- **Desire:** "Our practical, intensive 12-week Digital Marketing Masterclass gives you hands-on experience with real client budgets. Graduate with a portfolio, not just a certificate."
- **Action:** "Secure your spot in the next cohort. Only 15 seats available. [Enroll Now]"

## 2. Copy Guidelines
- **Focus on "You", not "We":** Avoid "We are a great agency." Use "You will get 3-6x ROI."
- **Readability:** Keep sentences under 15 words. Paragraphs under 3 lines. Use bullet points heavily. Reading level should be Grade 8.
- **Specifics over Generalities:** Instead of "We get you results," use "Our clients see an average 300% increase in lead volume within 60 days."

## 3. Email & WhatsApp Sequences
Because B2B sales cycles in Nigeria require trust-building, we will deploy a 5-part nurture sequence for anyone who fills out the database form but doesn't immediately close via WhatsApp.

### Sequence: The "SME Growth Catalyst" (5 Days)
**Trigger:** User submits a lead form for Web Design / Digital Marketing but does not book a meeting.

**Day 1: The Immediate Value (Sent 5 mins after inquiry)**
- *Subject:* Your website transformation roadmap
- *Body:* Thank them for reaching out. Acknowledge their specific goal (pulled from the form). Provide a free piece of immediate value (e.g., a PDF on "3 Reasons Websites Fail to Convert in 2026"). 
- *Call to Action (CTA):* "Reply to this email or click here to chat on WhatsApp so we can map out your specific strategy."

**Day 2: The Agitator & Authority Builder**
- *Subject:* Why your competitors are winning online...
- *Body:* Explain the difference between a "brochure" website and a "sales funnel." Highlight the cost of inaction. Mention Hephzibah's methodology.
- *CTA:* "Let's fix your funnel. Book a quick 15-min call."

**Day 3: The Case Study (Social Proof)**
- *Subject:* How [Client X] doubled their leads in 30 days
- *Body:* Tell a story. Start with the client's pain (similar to the prospect's pain). Explain the Hephzibah intervention. Show the concrete metric result.
- *CTA:* "Want these results for [Prospect's Company]? Let's chat."

**Day 4: The Pivot (If budget is an issue)**
- *Subject:* Not ready for a full redesign? Try this.
- *Body:* Introduce the Training Division. If they can't afford the ₦1M agency service, perhaps their marketing manager can take the ₦200k Digital Marketing course to learn how to do it in-house.
- *CTA:* "Check out our upcoming training cohorts."

**Day 5: The Breakup (Fear of Missing Out)**
- *Subject:* Closing the file on your project
- *Body:* "I haven't heard back, so I'm assuming improving your digital lead generation isn't a priority right now. I'll stop reaching out. If things change, you know where to find us."
- *CTA:* "If you *are* still interested, reply 'YES' to this email and I'll send over available meeting times."

## 4. Onboarding Email (For New Agency Clients)
**Goal:** Prevent buyer's remorse and set expectations.
- **Welcome Email:** Sent immediately after invoice payment. Outlines the 16-week timeline, what is expected of the client (assets, logins), and introduces the project manager. 
- **Tone:** Reassuring, highly organized, premium.
