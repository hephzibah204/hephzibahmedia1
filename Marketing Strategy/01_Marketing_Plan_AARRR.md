# Hephzibah Media: 12-Month Marketing Plan (AARRR Framework)

## 1. Executive Summary
Hephzibah Media is transforming from a premium informational website into a revenue-generating digital ecosystem. This 12-month marketing plan focuses on scaling qualified B2B SME leads from 10-15/month to 40-60/month, and launching a new training division with a target of 600-1,200 enrollments per year. 

**3 Big Bets:**
1. **Conversion-First Architecture:** Transforming service pages into high-converting sales pages with intelligent WhatsApp lead routing.
2. **SEO & Authority Building:** Dominating local and national search for web design and digital skills training in Nigeria.
3. **Training as Lead Gen:** Using the 12 new training courses to build an audience, generate immediate revenue (₦150k-₦300k ACV), and upsell high-ticket agency services.

**12-Month Outcome:** ₦324M - ₦576M+ in new revenue potential across agency services and the training division.

## 2. Strategic Frame
- **Category Claim:** The premier digital growth catalyst for Nigerian SMEs.
- **ICP Distilled:** Founders and Marketing Managers at B2B SMEs in Nigeria who are frustrated with "brochure" websites that don't generate leads or ROI.
- **Brand Voice:** Authoritative, Professional, Results-oriented, Premium. We speak in terms of ROI, automated lead generation, and business growth.

## 3. Current State
- **Current Spend:** Minimal. Project transformation is currently funded at ₦4.09M - ₦11.75M for development and content.
- **Team:** 1 Lead Developer, 1 Content/Copywriter, 0.5 Project Manager. Need to activate SEO specialist.
- **In-flight:** Website transformation Phase 2 (16 weeks).
- **Stuck:** Relying on a generic contact form with massive manual follow-up (2+ hours/day).

## 4. Acquisition (Strangers → Aware)
**Goal:** Drive highly qualified SME traffic to service pages and eager learners to training pages.
- **SEO (Primary):** Execute the Keyword Research Framework (Doc 02). Focus on local SEO ("web design lagos", "digital marketing agency nigeria") and course-specific SEO.
- **Content Marketing:** Publish comprehensive case studies and ROI-focused blog posts that demonstrate expertise. 
- **Social Media (LinkedIn & Twitter/X):** Share transformation before/afters, metrics, and thought leadership to attract B2B decision-makers.

## 5. Activation (Aware → Converted Lead)
**Goal:** Convert 10-15% of service page traffic into qualified leads.
- **Intelligent Routing:** Implement conditional logic on forms to route mobile users directly to WhatsApp for immediate conversation, and desktop users to the SQLite database.
- **High-Converting Service Pages:** Deploy the 9-section template (Doc 03) across all service offerings, emphasizing the Push/Pull dynamics (escaping bad websites, gaining an automated lead machine).
- **Frictionless Intake:** Use qualifying questions (budget, service type) to filter out the "cheap template" anti-persona.

## 6. Retention (Repeat Engagement)
**Goal:** Keep leads warm and maximize LTV through cross-selling.
- **Lead Nurture Sequence:** Develop an automated email and WhatsApp sequence for leads who don't close immediately, sharing case studies and ROI calculators.
- **Training Alumni Network:** Keep training graduates engaged with advanced courses or "Done-For-You" agency services.

## 7. Referral (Retained → Bring More)
**Goal:** Turn successful clients and students into advocates.
- **SME Referral Program:** Incentivize current agency clients to refer other business owners (e.g., offer a free SEO audit or landing page optimization for successful referrals).
- **Student Affiliate Program:** Allow training graduates to earn a commission for referring new students to the 12 digital skills courses.

## 8. Revenue (Monetization & Expansion)
**Goal:** Achieve ₦186M+ Year 1 revenue increase.
- **Agency Services:** Maintain high-ticket ACV (₦500k-₦2M) by refusing to compete on price, competing purely on ROI and conversion architecture.
- **New Service Line:** Actively push the new "Landing Pages & Sales Funnels" service as an entry-point offer with high margins.
- **Training Division:** Stack 12 courses at ₦150k-₦300k. Bundle courses for a discount or upsell agency services to businesses whose employees take the courses.

## 9. 90-Day Roadmap
- **Weeks 1-4 (Foundation):** Complete database setup, global address update (critical local SEO factor), and SEO keyword mapping.
- **Weeks 5-8 (Core Activation):** Launch the 5 priority service pages with WhatsApp routing. Begin aggressive A/B testing on forms.
- **Weeks 9-12 (Training & Expansion):** Launch the training hub and 12 course pages. Deploy the admin dashboard for lead management.

## 10. Open Decisions & Risks
- **CAC Baseline:** Need to establish true Customer Acquisition Cost once the new tracking is live.
- **Content Velocity:** Can 1 copywriter handle service pages, 12 training courses, AND ongoing blog content? May need to outsource or use AI assistance.
- **WhatsApp API Limits:** Ensure the WhatsApp routing uses an official API or robust fallback to prevent account bans during high lead volume.
