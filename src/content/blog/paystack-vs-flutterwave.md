      # Paystack vs Flutterwave: Which Should Your Nigerian Store Use?

      By Hephzibah Media Team · Published January 8, 2025
      You've launched your e-commerce store. Customers are ready to buy. But which payment gateway? Paystack and Flutterwave dominate Nigeria  but they're very different. Here's the side-by-side breakdown we use to advise clients.
    
  

  
    
      ## Transaction Fees: The Real Cost

      
Paystack: 1.5% + ?100 for card, 3.5% for bank transfer. Flutterwave: 1.4% + ?25 for card, 1.4% for bank transfer. On a ?50,000 order: Paystack costs ?850, Flutterwave costs ?724. Advantage: Flutterwave by ~?100200 per transaction. Over 1,000 orders monthly, that's ?100K200K difference.

      ## Settlement Speed

      
Paystack: T+1 (next business day) to your bank account. Flutterwave: T+23. If you need cash quickly (physical print inventory, urgent restocking), Paystack's speed matters. If you're confident in 48-hour float, Flutterwave is fine.

      ## Supported Payment Methods

      
Paystack: Card, bank transfer, USSD, QR, mobile money. Flutterwave: Card, bank transfer, USSD, mobile money, Crypto (Bitcoin, Ethereum). If your customers want to pay in crypto (uncommon in Nigeria today), Flutterwave is the only option. Otherwise, feature parity.

      ## Dashboard & Analytics

      
Paystack: Cleaner dashboard, easier reconciliation, better mobile app. Flutterwave: More complex interface, but deeper analytics (customer LTV, repeat purchase tracking). Winner: Paystack for simplicity, Flutterwave for data insights.

      ## International Settlement

      
Flutterwave routes to US bank accounts easier. Paystack requires additional KYC. If you're an export-focused Nigerian business wanting USD settlement, Flutterwave wins.

      ## Who Should Use Which

      
Paystack: Small to mid-size retailers (?010M monthly revenue), high transaction velocity, cash-dependent operations. Flutterwave: Higher-volume stores (?10M+), international expansion plans, customers paying via unconventional methods.
