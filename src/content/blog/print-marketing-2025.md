      # Print Marketing Still Works in Nigeria: 2025 Guide

      By Hephzibah Media Team · Published January 5, 2025
      Everyone talks about 'going digital.' But in Nigeria, print is still incredibly effective  especially when combined with digital. A high-quality business card at a networking event opens doors Google Ads can't. A branded banner at a trade show generates qualified foot traffic. Here's the strategic print playbook for 2025.
    
  

  
    
      ## Business Cards: Still Your Best Networking Tool

      
A premium business card (embossed, foil-stamped, specialty paper) positions you as professional and established. It stays on a prospect's desk for months. Cost: ?515 per card. Return: One client contract pays for 10,000 cards. Recommendation: 500 cards every 3 months.

      ## Flyers: Targeted Distribution Beats Broadcast

      
Don't print 10,000 flyers and hope. Instead: 500 flyers, hand-distributed at three high-traffic locations (office parks, malls, events) by your sales team. Include a QR code linking to a landing page  track clicks to measure ROI. Cost: ?50100 per flyer. Conversion: 25% is excellent for flyer campaigns.

      ## Banners at Trade Shows & Events

      
A trade show booth with a compelling banner, product samples, and a clear CTA ('Scan to get 20% off') generates 1020 leads per day. Cost: ?20K50K for the banner. Value: One converted lead from an event is worth ?500K+. Attend 23 high-quality events yearly.

      ## Large Format Printing for Visibility

      
A billboard for your agency costs ?200K500K monthly in high-traffic areas (Lekki, VI, GRA Ikeja). It's expensive, but if your CAC (customer acquisition cost) is below ?50K, a billboard that generates 1015 leads monthly pays for itself. Best for services (real estate, education, fintech).

      ## Packaging as Marketing

      
A branded box, tissue paper, or thank-you card in your order transforms a transaction into an experience. Customers photograph it for Instagram. Cost: ?100300 per order. Impact: Word-of-mouth multiplier of 23×.

      ## The Print + Digital Combo

      
QR codes on every print piece. Flyers link to a landing page. Banners include a unique promo code. Business cards have a QR to your LinkedIn. Print gets attention; digital captures the lead. Together, they outperform either channel alone.
