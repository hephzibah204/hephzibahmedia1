Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a landing page and a sales funnel?

A landing page is a single page designed for one action. A sales funnel is a complete system of multiple pages, emails, and automations that guide visitors from awareness to purchase.

How long does it take to build a landing page?

Single landing pages take 5-7 days. Complete sales funnels take 2-3 weeks depending on complexity and automation requirements.

Do you write the copy for the pages?

Yes! All our packages include professional conversion-focused copywriting. We research your audience, craft persuasive messaging, and optimize for conversions.

Can you integrate Paystack or Flutterwave?

Absolutely. We integrate all major Nigerian payment gateways including Paystack, Flutterwave, and direct bank transfer options.

How does the WhatsApp funnel work?

Visitors click a CTA that opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Their details are captured, and you receive an instant notification. We can also set up automated WhatsApp sequences.

Do you provide hosting for the pages?

We can host the pages on your existing hosting or recommend reliable options. Hosting is typically ₦10,000/month and includes performance monitoring.

What if the funnel doesn't convert?

We include optimization support and A/B testing. If initial results aren't meeting targets, we analyze data, identify bottlenecks, and make improvements at no extra cost.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans: 50% upfront, balance on completion. Custom arrangements available. Chat with us about what works for your budget.
